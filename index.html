Our Story

About Soccer for Change

Who we are

Soccer For Change is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded by high school students Vedanth Rao and Adel Dekhani to encourage the development of soccer in youth, along with all the positive benefits the sport promotes. We believe we can not only boost skills, but build important values such as confidence, teamwork, and leadership — and provide mentors who can guide students through soccer and their futures.

What we do

We provide accessible coaching through free programs. Our high school coaching staff has experience going through the young player pathway and knows what it takes to thrive as an elite soccer player. Your child will learn the skills they need to advance as a young player and build confidence, all while gaining a newfound passion for the sport.

Vedanth has 6 years of premier soccer experience, 2 years of select experience, and is a current player at Interlake High School. He has an ever-growing passion for the sport and loves sharing his skills with others.
