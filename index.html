About Soccer for Change

Who we are

Soccer For Change is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded by high school students Vedanth Rao and Adel Dekhani to encourage the development of soccer in youth, along with all the positive benefits the sport promotes. We believe we can not only boost skills, but build important values such as confidence, teamwork, and leadership — and provide mentors who can guide students through soccer and their futures.

What we do

We provide accessible coaching through free programs. Our high school coaching staff has experience going through the young player pathway and knows what it takes to thrive as an elite soccer player. Your child will learn the skills they need to advance as a young player and build confidence, all while gaining a newfound passion for the sport. We also seek to provide students with high school mentors who can help teach and support them as students.

Camps

Camp Registration

All camps are completely free for all participants. Held at Enatai Elementary School in Bellevue, WA.

⚽

Soccer Training Camp

Sign up to secure your spot in our free soccer camp! Open to all skill levels, with players grouped accordingly and coached by top-tier high school athletes from premier and select teams. Whether your child wants to sharpen skills with position-specific training or just have fun and learn the game, there's a place for everyone. Campers will build confidence, make friends, and be guided by mentors who care about growth on and off the field.

📅 October 4–25th, Saturdays 3–5 PM 📍 Enatai Elementary School, Bellevue, WA 💰 Free for all participants
🏅

Mid-Winter Break Camp

Designed for kids ages 7–12 of all skill levels. Participants train in small groups with focused drills and competitive games, while meeting new people and developing their skills. Our camps are taught by varsity and premier high school athletes who are passionate about developing the next generation.

📅 February 16–20th, 9 AM–11 AM 📍 Enatai Elementary, 10700 SE 25th St, Bellevue, WA 98004 💰 Free for all participants 👦 Ages 7–12
